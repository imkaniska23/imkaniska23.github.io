National Institute Of Technology, Durgapur

"Work maketh a man" - the motto of the college National Institute of Technology, Durgapur speaks what it stands for. A public engineering college deemed as an Institute of National Importance by the Indian Government, it strives to be among the top NITs in the country.

Model United Nations (MUN)

In MUN, students step into the shoes of ambassadors from U.N. member states to debate current issues on the organization's vast agenda. Student "delegates" in MUN prepare draft resolutions, plot strategy, negotiate with supporters and adversaries and resolve conflicts.

Our Objective Towards MUN

The Literary Circle's vision towards hosting MUN is to provide the students with a platform to test their communication skills and to improve upon them while getting exposed to the diplomatic ways of delegates.Moreover, it generates awareness regarding various global issues, their effects and the countermeasures required.
